# Project Specification

## Purpose

The app allows Warrnambool Trays & Trailers to plan daily workshop jobs, assign those jobs to staff, and track job time, breaks and notes.

## Users

### Boss
- Creates job plans by date
- Assigns multiple numbered jobs to each staff member
- Views live staff/job status
- Corrects missed clockings
- Exports CSV backups

### Staff
- Selects their name
- Clocks in/out
- Starts, pauses and finishes jobs
- Starts/ends breaks
- Adds job notes
- Reviews and submits their day

## Screens

### Home
- Boss View
- Staff View

### Boss View
- Job Planner
- Live View
- Summary
- Templates

### Staff View
- Staff selector
- Clock controls
- Job list
- Notes
- Submit day

## Version 1.0 target

The first live version should be simple, stable and usable by the company.
